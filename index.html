🔄 What the Meetings Funnel Tells Us

Structural insight: fresh meetings build pipeline, recurring meetings service existing clients — two distinct levers
🌱 Fresh Meetings = Future AUM

🔁 Recurring = Wallet Deepening
Recurring meetings don't grow AUM — they protect it. The teams with high recurring but low fresh meeting ratios (Madhup Patet, Chinmay) are in servicing mode, not growth mode. Rebalance RM time.
📐 Target: 60% Fresh / 40% Recurring
The data suggests the optimal mix for AUM growth is to keep fresh meeting share above 60% of total meetings. Track fresh vs recurring ratio per RM monthly as a leading indicator.
